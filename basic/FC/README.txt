F/C/H
     The follow information is reproduced from:

     "C_F_H (Fluorographene): Sandeep Kumar Singh, S. Goverapet Srinivasan, M. Neek-Amal, S. Costamagna, Adri C. T. van Duin, and F. M. Peeters Phys. Rev. B 87, 104114 (2013)."
